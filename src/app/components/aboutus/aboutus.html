WHO WE ARE

The NYPD Desi Society is a professional organization representing South Asian members of the New York Police Department. We are committed to fostering unity, mentorship, and community engagement while upholding the highest standards of public service.

Our society provides a platform for cultural representation, professional development, and meaningful collaboration within the department and the communities we serve.

Our Mission

To support, empower, and represent South Asian officers within the NYPD while promoting leadership, service, and community partnership.

Our Vision

To build a strong and unified network that strengthens representation, advances professional growth, and enhances trust between law enforcement and diverse communities.

OUR HISTORY

Founded to create a space of unity and representation, the NYPD Desi Society was established to support South Asian officers within the department. Over the years, the organization has grown into a respected professional network focused on mentorship, outreach, and service.

Through community engagement initiatives, cultural events, and leadership development, the Society continues to expand its impact while honoring the values that define the NYPD.
